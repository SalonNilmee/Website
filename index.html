Our Professional Story

Defining Beauty in Gampaha

Founded in 2011, Salon Nilmee has spent over 15 years establishing itself as Gampaha’s premier destination for sophisticated grooming and transformative beauty care. Led by the vision of Mrs. Nilmini Thennakoon, our studio blends professional-grade artistry with a deeply personalized touch. We believe that true beauty lies in the details—from precision cuts to advanced skincare rituals. Our legacy is built on trust, excellence, and an unwavering commitment to helping every client reveal their most confident, radiant self.
